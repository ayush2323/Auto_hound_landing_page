Reliable Buying Solution for High Performing Teams

MyAutoHound sets you up for success with a hyper-productive auction bot that ensures a listing matches your strict buying criteria before passing it along to your buying team.

Set your buying criteria

With over 33 buying criteria options, MyAutoHound will change the way you source online. Once you set up your filters, the tool runs itself. No more reading hundreds of condition reports per day.

Save time & overhead

The MyAutoHound engine runs 24/7 hunting for the listings that fit your buying criteria. From history report to damage disclosures, your team doesn’t have to waste time analyzing listings or condition reports anymore.

We will send you SMS messages and/or desktop notifications when a live listing matches your buying criteria. Let our tool do the research and you do the buying.
